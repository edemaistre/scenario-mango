The part that scales

Automate it: agents · MCP · Skills.

Nobody clicks buttons. Scenario is exposed over MCP (the open protocol agents speak), so an agent in your stack watches your PIM, and when a new SKU lands it runs the entire chain end to end, then publishes the SKU-keyed assets. Skills encode your house casting, art direction and acceptance rubric, so every run is on-brand.

1 New SKUPackshot lands in your PIM.
2 GenerateAgent calls Scenario over MCP: master, try-on, lifestyle, video, localized + social cuts.
3 SkillsHouse casting, art direction and the fidelity rubric applied automatically.
4 QA gateAuto-checks + human approve-before-publish on the hard cases.
5 PublishSKU-keyed assets pushed to PDP, social and DAM.

on new_sku(packshot):

  clip = scenario.video(shots)  # two-keyframe walk

Unit economics, with the sources on the page.
